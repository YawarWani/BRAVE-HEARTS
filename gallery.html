Our Visual Diary

A glimpse into the untouched beauty of Kashmir

Gulmarg

Gulmarg, the "Meadow of Flowers," is a majestic hill station nestled in the Pir Panjal Range. Famous for its world-class skiing, pristine snow-covered landscapes, and the exhilarating Gondola ride, it is a paradise for adventurers and peace-seekers alike.

Sonmarg

Sonmarg, meaning the "Meadow of Gold," is adorned with breathtaking glaciers, alpine meadows, and serene lakes. It serves as the gateway to Ladakh and offers spectacular views of the Himalayan peaks touching the clear blue skies.
